PRIVACY POLICY
EXPIRA PRO

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Shokhrukh Akhmatov ("Developer", "we", or "us") collects, uses, and processes personal information in connection with the Expira Pro mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy in compliance with applicable laws, including data protection regulations in Tajikistan and, where relevant, international standards like GDPR.

1. Information We Collect

  • Language preferences and notification settings.
  • Device information (e.g., OS version, device model, unique device identifiers).
  • Advertising identifiers (processed by third-party services like Yandex Mobile Ads SDK).
  • Usage data (e.g., app interactions) to improve performance.

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as health or financial information unless explicitly required for future features, in which case we will obtain consent.

2. Legal Basis for Processing

We process data based on your consent (e.g., for ads and notifications), legitimate interests (e.g., app functionality and improvements), or as necessary for contract performance (e.g., providing reminders).

3. How We Use Data

  • To provide core features like reminders and shelf life calculations.
  • To analyze and improve app performance and user experience.
  • To display personalized advertisements via third-party SDKs.
  • For security and fraud prevention.

4. Data Sharing

We may share advertising-related data with third parties like Yandex, in accordance with their privacy policy (available at: https://yandex.com/legal/privacy/). We do not sell your data. Data may be shared with service providers for hosting or analytics, under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Storage and Security

Currently, data is stored locally on your device. Future cloud features may involve secure server storage with encryption and access controls. We implement reasonable security measures to protect data from unauthorized access, loss, or breach.

6. International Transfers

If data is transferred internationally (e.g., to Yandex servers), we ensure compliance with applicable regulations, such as using standard contractual clauses or adequacy decisions.

7. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in some jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover such data, it will be deleted immediately.

8. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict your data, or object to processing. Contact us to exercise these rights. You may also uninstall the App to remove locally stored data. For ad-related choices, opt out via device settings or Yandex tools.

9. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described (e.g., until app uninstallation) or as required by law. Anonymized data may be kept longer for analytics.

10. Data Breaches

In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and authorities as required by law (e.g., within 72 hours under GDPR-like rules).

11. Changes

We may update this Policy periodically. Changes will be posted here, and significant updates may be notified in-app. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
